## What is ShiftControl?

ShiftControl is an IT management platform built for teams that don't have (or don't want) a full-time IT department. If you're a founder managing logins in a spreadsheet, an office manager juggling five admin consoles, or a first engineer who inherited IT — ShiftControl is for you.

At its core, ShiftControl does four things:

1. **Centralizes identity** — Every person in your company gets one identity that controls access to everything. No more fragmented accounts across dozens of SaaS tools.
2. **Automates access** — When someone joins, they get the right apps on day one based on their role, department, and team. When they leave, access is revoked everywhere — automatically.
3. **Controls SaaS costs** — See exactly what your organization spends on software, per user and per app. Find unused licenses before they renew.
4. **Strengthens security** — MFA enforcement, OAuth permission auditing, and admin oversight across your entire stack.

## How It Works

ShiftControl connects to your identity providers and manages everything through a single interface.

<ConditionalContent modes={["jc-only"]}>

Your organization uses **JumpCloud** as the identity provider. ShiftControl sits on top of JumpCloud and provides a simpler, more powerful admin experience. Changes you make in ShiftControl are pushed to JumpCloud via API in real time, which propagates them to every connected application.

</ConditionalContent>

<ConditionalContent modes={["jc-google"]}>

Your organization uses **JumpCloud** as the primary identity provider with **Google Workspace** connected as a secondary directory. ShiftControl manages both from a single interface — user profiles, group memberships, security settings, and admin access flow to both directories simultaneously. JumpCloud handles authentication and SSO while Google Workspace manages email, Google Groups, and Google-specific access.

</ConditionalContent>

<ConditionalContent modes={["google-only"]}>

Your organization uses **Google Workspace** as the identity provider. ShiftControl manages your Google directory, admin roles, group memberships, and application access from a single interface that's easier to use than the Google Admin Console.

</ConditionalContent>
