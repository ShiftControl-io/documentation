## Overview

The group detail page answers the questions you ask during access reviews:
- "Who is in this group and how did they get there?"
- "What apps does this group grant access to?"
- "What rules control membership?"
- "Is this group syncing to Google Workspace?"

Click any group name in the [Group Management](/using-shiftcontrol/Groups/Group-management/) list to open it.

<ModeScreenshot path="Groups/Group-detail" alt="Group detail page showing membership, rules, and app assignments" />

## Membership Tabs

The group detail page has tabs showing how members are added:

### Dynamic Rules

Shows rules that automatically add/remove users based on profile attributes. For example: "When Department equals Engineering" adds everyone in the Engineering department. When someone transfers out, they're automatically removed.

<ModeScreenshot path="Groups/Group-dynamic-assignments" alt="Dynamic membership rules" />

### Nested Groups

Shows other groups whose members are included in this group. If you nest the "Engineering" group inside "All Technical Staff", every engineer becomes a member of both.

### Individual Assignments

Shows users who were manually added to the group — useful for one-off exceptions or contractors who don't match any dynamic rule.

## The Sidebar

The right sidebar gives you quick counts and details:

### Rules Tab
Summary of active rules — how many dynamic, nested, and individual rules are configured.

### Users Tab
<ModeScreenshot path="Groups/Group-sidebar-users" alt="Sidebar showing group members" />

Lists all current members and shows *how* each was added (dynamic rule, nested group, or individual assignment). Hover over the icons for details.

### Apps Tab
<ModeScreenshot path="Groups/Group-sidebar-apps" alt="Sidebar showing assigned apps" />

Shows which applications are assigned to this group. Every member of this group gets access to these apps.

## Common Scenarios

### Access review — checking who has access to a sensitive app

You need to verify who has access to AWS Production. Open the "AWS Production Access" group, check the **Users** sidebar tab to see every member, and the **Apps** sidebar to confirm which apps this group grants. Check the **Dynamic** tab to verify the membership rule hasn't been overly broadened.

### Understanding why a user has unexpected access

A user has access to a tool they shouldn't. Open the relevant group from their user detail sidebar, check the **Dynamic** tab to see if a rule is matching too broadly, and the **Nested** tab to see if they're inheriting membership from another group.

### Verifying Google Sync status

<ConditionalContent modes={["jc-google", "google-jc", "google-only"]}>

Check the **Details** tab for the Google Sync toggle and email address. If sync is enabled, every membership change in ShiftControl is pushed to the corresponding Google Group automatically.

</ConditionalContent>
