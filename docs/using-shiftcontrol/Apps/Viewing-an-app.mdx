## Overview

The app detail page is where you go to understand an application's full footprint:
- "Who has access to this app and through which groups?"
- "What does this subscription cost and when does it renew?"
- "How is SSO configured?"
- "Who owns this app?"

Click any app name in the [App Management](/using-shiftcontrol/Apps/App-management/) list to open it.

<ModeScreenshot path="Apps/App-detail" alt="App detail page showing configuration and assignments" />

## The Details Tab

Shows the app's basic information:
- **App name** — Display name across ShiftControl and the employee portal
- **Primary admin** — Person responsible for day-to-day management
- **Budget owner** — Person responsible for costs and renewals
- **Owning department** — Which department owns this app
- **Description** — Visible to employees in their app dashboard
- **Bookmark URL** — (Bookmarks only) Where users are sent when they click the app

## The Subscription Tab

Shows plan details and costs that feed into the [App Spend Dashboard](/using-shiftcontrol/Dashboards/app-spend/):
- Plan duration, billing cycle, and renewal date
- Per-user or flat-rate cost
- Platform fees and notes

This is where you check before renewal conversations — see exactly what you're paying and when the charge hits.

## The Assignments Tab

Shows who has access and how:

- **Groups sub-tab** — Which groups are assigned to this app. Every member of those groups has access.
- **Individual sub-tab** — Users assigned directly, outside of any group.

## The SSO Tab (SSO Apps Only)

For SSO SAML and SSO OIDC apps, shows the JumpCloud identity provider configuration:
- IDP public certificate
- SSO config metadata (downloadable for the app vendor)
- SAML assertion settings and attribute mappings

## Context Menu Actions

Click the **⋮ menu** on any app row in the App Management list:

- **Edit** — Open the app for editing
- **Delete** — Remove the app from ShiftControl (disabled for the ShiftControl app itself)

<ConditionalContent modes={["jc-only", "jc-google", "google-jc"]}>

Additional actions for JumpCloud-managed apps:
- **Regenerate Secret** — Rotate OIDC client secrets (OIDC apps only)
- **Convert to SSO App** — Upgrade a bookmark to SSO (bookmark apps only, disabled if directory sync is active)
- **Convert to bookmark app** — Downgrade an SSO app to a bookmark (SAML apps only, disabled if directory sync is active)

</ConditionalContent>

Bulk actions: Select multiple apps and choose **Delete** to remove them in bulk.

## Common Scenarios

### Preparing for a renewal negotiation

Open the app, check the **Subscription** tab for the current cost and renewal date. Check **Assignments > Groups** to see how many people actually have access. Cross-reference with usage data to build your negotiation case.

### Investigating why someone has access

Open the app, check the **Assignments** tab. If access is group-based, click into the group to understand its membership rules. If it's an individual assignment, you can see who added it.

### Verifying SSO is configured correctly

Open the app, go to the **SSO** tab. Verify the IdP URL, certificate, and assertion settings match what the vendor expects.
