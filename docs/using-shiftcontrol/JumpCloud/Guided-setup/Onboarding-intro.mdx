<p style={{fontSize: "large"}}>The white-glove setup path for organizations onboarding ShiftControl on JumpCloud.</p>

<Admonition type="tip" title="On Google Workspace only?">
    You don't need a guided session. [Onboarding](/getting-started/Onboarding/) gets you a working ShiftControl org in a few minutes.
</Admonition>

If you're standing up ShiftControl on JumpCloud (with or without Google Workspace alongside), this is your path. The next three pages cover what to prepare **before** the call, what we walk through **during** it, and what to do **after**. The guided session exists because there are a few hands-on configuration steps that go faster when we walk through them together.
