There are two paths to onboarding ShiftControl, and the right one depends on which identity provider your organization runs.

<Admonition type="tip" title="Google Workspace only?">
    If your organization uses Google Workspace as its only identity provider, you can [onboard yourself in minutes](/onboarding/Self-service-onboarding/) — no setup call required. The self-service flow walks you through sign-up, domain validation, OAuth authorization, and provisioning, and lands you on a ready-to-use ShiftControl org.
</Admonition>

If your organization uses JumpCloud (with or without Google Workspace alongside), follow the guided path on the next three pages: things to do **before** the onboarding call, what we cover **during** it, and what to do **after**. The guided path exists because configuring JumpCloud as your IdP and trusting it from the Google Admin console has steps that are easier to do live together.

This guide will walk you through those steps before, during, and after your ShiftControl onboarding session.
